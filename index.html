About Me

Hi, I'm Nicolas Diacono, a Systems Technician and qualified Electrician based in Christchurch, New Zealand. I work in Microinverter Engineering, combining hands-on experience with technical insight to support solar technologies.

With a strong foundation in residential and commercial PV installations, I've transitioned into automation, productivity tools, and test case generation using Python. I enjoy building intuitive GUI tools that streamline workflows, including AI-powered applications for data analysis and predictive maintenance.

I'm also expanding into industrial control systems with PLC ladder logic programming, currently completing a course at Udemy's PLC Dojo to strengthen my automation and process control skills.

Outside of work, I'm passionate about fitness, surfing, and outdoor activities. I love learning new technologies and spending time with friends and family.
